## The Original

### [OpenClaw](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw)
The pioneer of the ecosystem. A free and open-source autonomous AI agent designed to be your personal assistant.
- **Key Features**: Proactive task management, persistent memory, messaging platform integrations (WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack).
- **Core Value**: An AI that "actually does things" rather than just chatting.

## Ecosystem Variants

### Frameworks & Core

#### [PaeanClaw](https://github.com/paean-ai/paeanclaw)
A modular and extensible implementation focusing on specialized skill sets and payment integrations.
- **Highlights**: Strong support for MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers, stablecoin payment flows, and enterprise-ready agent patterns.

#### [0Claw (ZeroClaw)](https://github.com/paean-ai/0claw)
A high-performance implementation designed for efficiency and speed.
- **Highlights**: Focuses on minimal latency and low-resource consumption.

---

### Mobile & Portable

#### [AnyClaw](https://github.com/paean-ai/anyclaw)
The bridge to mobile autonomy. Designed to bring the Claw experience to Android and other portable environments.
- **Highlights**: Bundled Linux environments, non-root execution, and seamless mobile assistant capabilities.

---

### Lightweight & Fast

#### [NanoClaw](https://github.com/qwibitai/nanoclaw)
An ultra-lightweight and secure alternative focusing on containerization.
- **Highlights**: Small binary footprint, Linux container isolation for agents, and a "security-first" approach to autonomous tasks.
